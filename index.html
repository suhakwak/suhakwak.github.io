I am an associate professor of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science at POSTECH, and a faculty member of POSTECH Computer Vision Lab. I did a post-doc with Ivan Laptev and Jean Ponce in the WILLOW team at the Department of Computer Science of the École Normale Supérieure and Inria Paris. I completed my BS and PhD at POSTECH where I worked with Joon Hee Han and Bohyung Han.

My research lies in computer vision and machine learning. I currently work in particular on label-efficient learning, adaptive machine learning, metric learning, action and activity recognition, and learning reliable models for visual perception.
